Ernest O. Jephthah

Founder · Sombre9 Tech Ltd

My journey began in my mother’s bookshop, where novels became teachers. Writers like David Eddings shaped how I understood imagination, description, and natural storytelling long before technology entered my life.

Loss and responsibility interrupted creativity. I worked in construction to survive, supporting family while design lived quietly through blueprints and structure.

Learning became a turning point. Through free platforms and self-study, I explored artificial intelligence — understanding systems, models, and prompts. AI became a tool for expression, not a replacement for thought.

Web development followed. Communities like OG Tech Ltd taught me structure and logic. Combining fundamentals with AI allowed me to design, debug, and build with intention.

What started as small visuals evolved into Sombre9 Tech Ltd — uniting design, web, and storytelling. Writing continues under the pen name Âme Sombre.

This journey continues. Every project is built with purpose — blending creativity, technology, and clarity to create work that lasts.
